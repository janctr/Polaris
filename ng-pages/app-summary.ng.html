App Summary

POLARIS - Pacific Operations Logistics and Readiness Information System

The goal of POLARIS is to provide the USINDOPACOM J4 an adaptable platform that provides visibility of logistics readiness, current operations, and future operations. The primary goals are to answer the operational logistics key visibility questions aligned to the 7 functions of joint logistics. Our approach is to create a cohesive mesh of automated data with the capability of correcting/updating lagging data sets that are paired and tailored to the J4 Divisions and Branches according to their unique reporting requirements set by the J4 Director. By answering the Operational Logistics Key Visibility questions in a layered approach focusing on readines (APOD/SPOD status, Pre-positioned material, Asset Readiness, Classes of Supply, and Posture) with Current operations metrics (In-Transit-Visbility, Force Closure, and Logistics Current Operations. Once the here and now have been answered the application can focus on future operations through modeling & simulations against Operational requirements to generate sustainment plans provide planners and decision makers visibility across a fast, agile, and adaptable Joint Logistics Enterprise.

Priority Focus Areas

  • Class III - Inventory, Warehousing, and Supply Chain
  • Class V - Inventory, Warehousing, and Supply Chain
  • Deployment & Distribution - Asset Visibility, APOD/SPODs, In-Transit-Visibility, and Logistics Operations
  • Sustainment - Pre-positioned Materials
